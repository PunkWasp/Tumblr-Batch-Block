Instructions:

  1. Navigate to the 'Edit appearance' page for your blog.
  2. Click the button with the action you would like to take.

Please note:

  • Both blocking and exporting the blocklist may take some time.
  • You can continue to surf the web in another tab while the app works.
  • If you are exporting your blocklist, it will pop up no matter which tab you are in.
